Bio

Colter Wall was born on June 27, 1995 in Swift Current, Canada. Wall first picked up the guitar when he was 12 years old, and by the time he was 13, he'd decided he wanted to make music his career. He first started gaining traction in the United States after the release of the albulm "Song of the Plains".
